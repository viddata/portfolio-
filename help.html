FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about listing, selling, and renting properties on our platform.

How do I list my property?

To list a property, click on the List Property button in the top right navbar. Choose a pricing plan that fits your requirements (Owner, Broker, or Builder plan) and fill out the property listing request form. Once submitted, it will directly request approval on WhatsApp.

Are there any listing charges?

Yes, we have custom pricing options tailored to provide max exposure: - Individual Owners: ₹999 per listing. - Brokers: ₹4,999 per month for up to 10 active listings. - Builders: Custom project packaging.

How do I pay my listing charges?

Once you submit your property request via our form, our team will review the details and reach out to you directly on WhatsApp. We will share a secure UPI/Payment QR code or bank transfer details to process your payment quickly and set your listing live.

How will buyers contact me?

When buyers submit inquiries on our portal for your listed property, their requests are automatically forwarded to you. Premium plans feature dedicated direct buttons (like direct WhatsApp connect) for interested clients to reach out immediately.
